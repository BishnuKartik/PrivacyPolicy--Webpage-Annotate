📄 Privacy Policy

Webpage Annotate v1.0.0
Last updated: May 21, 2026

Webpage Annotate – Add Highlights & Notes is designed with your privacy as the highest priority. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All annotations, notes, and settings remain exclusively on your own device.

🔒 Data Collection – None

We do not collect any information about you, your browsing habits, your annotations, or your device. There are no analytics, no crash reporting, no telemetry, and no remote servers. The extension has no backend – it operates entirely inside your browser.

💾 Where your data lives

All highlights, notes, color choices, style settings, and dark mode preference are stored locally using chrome.storage.local (or the equivalent Edge API). This storage is sandboxed and only accessible by this extension. If you uninstall the extension, all stored data is automatically removed by the browser.

📤 Data export / deletion

You can export all your annotations as a plain text file using the Export button in the popup. To delete your data:

  • Remove individual highlights via the popup or the toolbar on the page.
  • Clear all annotations using the Clear All button in the popup footer.
  • Uninstall the extension – the browser automatically removes all stored data.

👤 Third‑party services

This extension does not use any third‑party analytics, crash reporting, advertising networks, or CDNs that track users. The source code is self‑contained and does not load any remote scripts.

🧒 Children's privacy

The extension does not knowingly collect any personal information from any person, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, there is no risk of inadvertent collection.

🔄 Changes to this Privacy Policy

Any future updates to this policy will be reflected in a new version of the extension on the Chrome Web Store and Microsoft Edge Add‑ons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised accordingly.
